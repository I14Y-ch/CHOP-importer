# Title
Swiss classification of surgical interventions (CHOP) - Systematic index - Version {YEAR}
# Description
The Swiss classification of surgical interventions (CHOP) is used to record interventions and procedures as part of the inpatient hospital survey (SpiGes).
The CHOP is published in various formats (PDF, CSV, JSON and print) and is available in German, French and Italian. The CHOP is not issued in English.
